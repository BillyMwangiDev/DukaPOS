## ğŸ›’ Making Sales

### 1. Adding Items
- **Scan**: Simply scan a product barcode; it will be added to the cart automatically.
- **Search**: Type the product name in the search bar and click the product card.
- **Quantity**: Click the `+` or `-` buttons on the cart item to adjust quantity.

### 2. Checking Out
- Click the large green **Pay** button.
- Select the payment method:
    - **Cash**: Enter the amount received to calculate change.
    - **Mobile (M-Pesa)**: Use "Buy Goods" or "Paybill" and enter the reference code.
    - **Credit**: Assign the sale to a registered customer.
- Click **Finalize & Receipt**.

### 3. Holding Orders
If a customer needs to step away, click **Hold Order**. You can retrieve it later from the **Held Orders** tab without losing your current progress.
